Our Story

10 years ago, I walked in to a bakery store, after an intense phone call that ends my relationship with the love of my life. At list that was what I thought.The smell of cakes backing in the oven, the sound of people drinking their coffee and talking to each other, made me fill that everything is going to be OK. So, I bought a cup of hot coco and a piece of cheesecake and decided that on day, I will open my own bakery, for all of those who had their heart broke, or just need a place with good pastry to be.
